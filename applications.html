Applications

Retail Vendor

Looking to sell your product? Whether you're selling handcrafted plushies or thrifted articles of clothing, apply to find a spot to sell at this upcoming market!

Food Vendor

Looking to serve a series of tasteful food? We would love to share your delicious dishes with the rest of the community! If you're interested in sharing your food with the families and groups that attend the market, apply to find a good spot to share at the next upcoming event!

Entertainment

Want to share a wonderful talent with the community? From carnival games to henna art to hosting photoshoots, we are always looking for new additions to make the market more exciting and eventful. Fill out an entertainment application to find a spot to share a memorable experience with the rest of the community!

Market Crew

Interested in being a part of our 702 Market crew? Looking to grow our crew of energetic and hard-working members to continue to assist with event service. If you're interested in helping with keeping the market safe and organized for all the patrons, considering apply to join the market crew position!
